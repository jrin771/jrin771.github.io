Welcome to the world's largest GitHub Repository on LLMs + Robotics. Why did you make this?

I think the intersection of LLMs and Robotics is and will continue to be very cool.

Since this field is so cool, there should be more organized research, education, and outreach.

This is my contribution.

Furthermore, this is also part of an ongoing final project I’m doing for a class at Stanford called PWR1KA: The Rhetoric of Innovation.

Why do you only allow PRs?

I could’ve included options for emails or text messages or something like that but here are some of my objections:

Equity. If you have an idea it goes into the same queue as every other idea.

Organization.Instead of having to sort between 20 different places it’s all in one area.

Efficiency. Same structure and same location = much easier for me to make mass changes.

Can I include this on my top 10 list of breakthroughs that will replace ChatGPT this week?

Get a real job.
