What is AI ASCII Art?

Hi, my name is Jacob Rintamaki. I’m a junior at Stanford working for Elad Gil.

This art was made with large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT.

These models are trained to “predict the next token [word]”, so it is surprising that they are able to produce complex, visually compelling shapes.

That type of art is called calligrams, and Ophira/Janus were two of the earliest pioneers of this form with LLMs.
