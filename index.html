My thoughts on AGI

First off, "AGI is a vibe" not a convoluted proof (although those are cool)

Second I think that if the vibe of AGI is "this basically feels like a person" then it'll happen before 2030

My intuition for this is multimodality, recent explosions of public interest, funding, and usage in AI, and how GPUs are still getting better for now.

For ASI (Aritifical Superintelligence) I am much more bearish. Simulating what a person feels like vs being smarter than every human who is or has ever lived are very different tasks.

It would be very interesting (and perhaps deadly) to be proven wrong, but I don't think ASI will be here this decade.
